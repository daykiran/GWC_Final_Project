Featured through the experience pages we have a series of collected interviews and articles from people of color around the Chicago Land Area. The majority of interviewees are in either S.T.E.M. Academias as teachers and students or in S.T.E.M. Workplaces holding employee or manager level positions. All interviews are aimed to cover the experiences of people of color, for their stories to be heard and learned from--each holding a unique perspective.
