Experiences of POC

This is a collection of interviews that we collected from people of color in three different enviornments: Corporate, School, and in Chicago. Many of the people we interviwed also identify with other minority groups, whther that be gender, religion, or sexuality. These interviews can educate people about the discrimination poc face and also give advice for fellow people of color.
